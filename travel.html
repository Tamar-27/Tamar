Mombasa, Kenya

Mombasa is a beautiful coastal city known for its stunning beaches, rich culture, and historical sites like Fort Jesus.

Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

The bustling capital of Ethiopia, Addis Ababa is known for its rich history, coffee culture, and being a gateway to the country's beautiful landscapes.

Vodice, Croatia

Croatia offers stunning coastlines along the Adriatic Sea and is famous for its medieval architecture, including the ancient city of Dubrovnik.

Valleta, Malta

Malta, a Mediterranean gem, is known for its historical fortresses, vibrant culture, and stunning blue waters.

Gothenburg, Sweden

Sweden boasts beautiful landscapes ranging from modern cities like Stockholm to the serene forests and northern lights in the Arctic Circle.
